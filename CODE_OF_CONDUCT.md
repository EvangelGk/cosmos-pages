# Code of Conduct — Cosmos Sport Arena

## Our commitment
Cosmos Sport Arena is a level playing field. We commit to a fair, respectful, and discrimination-free environment for every participant, juror, and staff member, in line with **Law 3304/2005** (equal treatment) and **Law 4443/2016** (anti-discrimination directive transposition).

## Anti-discrimination
Decisions are made **solely on the merit of submissions**. We prohibit discrimination on the basis of: gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, race, color, ethnic or national origin, religion, age, disability, family or marital status, language, political or other opinion, socio-economic background, university or any other characteristic protected by Greek and EU law.

## Fairness in evaluation
- **Anonymized first-pass review:** name, gender markers, age, university and photo are masked before scoring.
- **Structured rubric:** identical criteria, weights and scales for every submission.
- **Calibration round:** jurors score one shared submission, deltas are reviewed before live scoring begins.
- **Conflict of interest:** jurors must recuse from submissions by relatives, classmates, employees, students or anyone they have personally taught/supervised in the last 24 months.
- **Right to human review:** any candidate may request a human re-review of an automated score (GDPR Art. 22).
- **Audit trail:** every score, comment and override is logged immutably for at least 12 months.

## Expected behavior
- Use respectful, professional language in all communications.
- Submit your own original work; cite all sources.
- Respect intellectual property of Cosmos Sport and other participants.
- Do not attempt to identify or contact other participants outside the platform without their consent.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, bullying, slurs, threats — online or offline.
- Plagiarism, AI-generated submissions misrepresented as original (declare AI assistance honestly).
- Tampering with the leaderboard, vote-rigging, fake accounts.
- Sharing private submissions outside the jury room.
- Doxing or unauthorized publication of personal data.

## Reporting
- **Confidential channel:** ethics@cosmos-sport.example.com
- **Anonymous channel:** [whistleblowing portal] (Law 4990/2022 compliant)
- All reports are handled within 7 calendar days. Retaliation against reporters is itself a disciplinary offense.

## Enforcement
Sanctions scale with severity: warning · removal of submission · disqualification from current contest · ban from future contests · referral to authorities.

## Scope
This Code applies to: the Arena platform, livestream events, official social channels, jury rooms, and any communication where a participant identifies as belonging to the Arena community.
