# Privacy Notice — Cosmos Sport Arena

**Last updated:** 2025 · **Language:** EL / EN
**Controller:** Cosmos Sport S.A. (the "Company")
**DPO contact:** dpo@cosmos-sport.example.com

This Privacy Notice is provided pursuant to **Articles 12–14 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR)** and **Greek Law 4624/2019** for personnel-selection processing (Hellenic DPA Guidance 1/2017 & 1/2024).

---

## 1. Who we are
Cosmos Sport S.A., acting as **Data Controller**, operates the "Cosmos Sport Arena" platform for talent acquisition through structured business challenges.

## 2. What data we process
| Category | Examples | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Identification | Full name, email, phone (optional) | Provided by candidate |
| Educational / Professional | University, year of study, CV, portfolio | Provided by candidate |
| Submission content | Strategy decks, briefs uploaded | Provided by candidate |
| Technical | IP address, browser, device, session logs | Automatic (security) |
| Consent records | Timestamps of consents granted/withdrawn | System-generated |

We do **not** process special-category data (Art. 9 GDPR) for selection purposes.

## 3. Purposes & lawful bases (Art. 6 GDPR)
| Purpose | Lawful basis |
|---|---|
| Process the application & evaluate the submission | Art. 6(1)(b) — pre-contractual measures |
| Retain in talent pool for future opportunities | Art. 6(1)(a) — consent (opt-in, withdrawable) |
| Newsletter / marketing about future contests | Art. 6(1)(a) — consent |
| Security, fraud prevention, audit logs | Art. 6(1)(f) — legitimate interest |
| Compliance with legal obligations (e.g. tax for prizes) | Art. 6(1)(c) — legal obligation |

## 4. Automated decision-making
First-pass scoring may use rule-based automation. Candidates have the **right to human intervention, to express their point of view and to contest the decision** (Art. 22 GDPR). No final selection is made solely by automated means.

## 5. Recipients
- Internal HR & evaluation panel members (need-to-know, RBAC).
- External jury members under signed NDA + DPA.
- IT / hosting provider (EU-region) under Art. 28 DPA.
- Public authorities only where legally required.

## 6. International transfers
By default, all data stays within the **EEA**. If a transfer outside the EEA becomes necessary, it will be based on **Standard Contractual Clauses (2021/914)** plus a Transfer Impact Assessment.

## 7. Retention
| Dataset | Retention |
|---|---|
| Non-selected candidates (no talent-pool consent) | **6 months** post-contest |
| Talent pool (with consent) | **24 months**, re-confirmed annually |
| Selected candidates → hired | Per HR record-retention policy |
| Security & access logs | **12 months** |
| Consent records | Lifetime of relationship + 5 years (proof of consent) |

After retention, data is **securely erased or anonymized**.

## 8. Your rights (Arts. 15–22 GDPR)
- **Access** — request a copy of your data.
- **Rectification** — correct inaccurate data.
- **Erasure** ("right to be forgotten").
- **Restriction** of processing.
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Objection** — to processing based on legitimate interest or marketing.
- **Withdraw consent** — at any time, without affecting prior lawfulness.
- **Lodge a complaint** with the **Hellenic Data Protection Authority** (www.dpa.gr · Kifissias 1-3, 11523 Athens · +30 210 6475600).

To exercise rights: **dpo@cosmos-sport.example.com** (response within 30 days, extendable by 60).

## 9. Security
See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). In summary: TLS 1.3, AES-256 at rest, RBAC, MFA, immutable audit logs, encrypted backups, ISO 27001/27701-aligned controls.

## 10. Children
The platform is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly process data of minors.

## 11. Updates
Material changes are notified by email and posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
