# Terms of Participation — Cosmos Sport Arena

**Effective:** 2025 · **Operator:** Cosmos Sport S.A. (the "Company")

By submitting an entry to any contest hosted on Cosmos Sport Arena (the "Platform"), the participant accepts these Terms.

## 1. Eligibility
- Natural persons, **at least 18 years old**, lawfully residing in the EU/EEA.
- Employees of the Company and their first-degree relatives may not participate in contests where they could obtain an unfair advantage.
- Teams: max 4 members; one team captain is the sole point of contact.

## 2. Submissions
- Submissions must be **original work** of the participant(s).
- AI-assisted work is permitted **only if disclosed** in the submission. Misrepresentation results in disqualification.
- Submissions must not infringe third-party rights, including IP, trademarks, or confidential information.
- Each submission must be in the format specified by the brief (file types, page limits, language).

## 3. Intellectual property
- The participant retains **ownership** of their submission and all underlying IP.
- The participant grants the Company a **non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide license** to:
  (a) display the submission on the Platform and at the official livestream event,
  (b) reference the submission in employer-branding materials, with clear attribution.
- For winning submissions only, an **option** to negotiate broader rights may be offered separately, in writing.
- The Company will not implement any submission's strategic content without a separate written agreement and fair compensation.

## 4. Evaluation
- Anonymized first-pass scoring (see [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)).
- Mixed jury: internal Company executives + external independent experts.
- Right to human re-review of automated scoring (GDPR Art. 22).
- The Company's decisions on disqualification are documented and reviewable by the DPO/legal team.

## 5. Prizes & recognition
- Recognition: badge on the Platform, feature in the livestream event, fast-track interview.
- Monetary prizes (if any) are stated per-contest. Tax obligations of the recipient are governed by Greek tax law; the Company will withhold and remit any applicable amounts.

## 6. Data
- The Company processes personal data per the [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.html).
- Public leaderboard shows only the participant-chosen display name and score. Real names are never published without explicit consent.

## 7. Conduct
- Participants must comply with the [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
- Violations may result in warning, removal of submission, disqualification, or ban from future contests.

## 8. Liability

## 10. Changes
The Company may amend these Terms with at least 14 days' notice prior to the start of any contest. Material changes during a running contest require participant re-acceptance.
